Days to Publication

Thu 09 August 2018 in posts
#python
An example demonstrating the use of webscraping techniques to collect article data from scientific journals, including publication date, received date, authors, title, etc. The data is analyzed using a simple linear regression model to predict the average number of days to publication (from received date to published date) from a few basic predictors.
